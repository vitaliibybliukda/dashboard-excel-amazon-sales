# Key Business Insights

## 📊 Finding #1: Electronics Dominates Revenue
- **Observation**: Electronics category generates 42% of total revenue ($1.03M)
- **Problem**: Profit margin is only 15% (vs 22% for Books)
- **Recommendation**: Renegotiate supplier contracts or adjust pricing strategy

## 📈 Finding #2: Strong Q4 Seasonality
- **Observation**: Q4 sales are 35% above annual average
- **Opportunity**: Launch pre-season marketing in Q3
- **Risk**: Inventory shortage during peak season
- **Recommendation**: Increase stock levels by 40% in October

## 🌍 Finding #3: West Region Growth
- **Observation**: West region shows 24% YoY growth
- **Concern**: Lowest repeat purchase rate (12%)
- **Recommendation**: Implement customer loyalty program
